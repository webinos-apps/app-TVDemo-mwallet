Each week, six amateur chefs cook from Monday until Friday against each other. Mondays cook the candidates their favorite dish, a starter on Tuesdays, Wednesdays a main course, main course and dessert on Thursday on Friday and two meals by a recipe of the moderating celebrity chef. At the end of each program chooses an expert who retires and who comes next, without knowing who cooked what dish.
